Mason Enters Luther College

My name is Mason, and I welcome you to my website! In recent weeks I have found myself beckoned into the fold of collegiate life; often, I find myself reminiscing over the events which led to this moment. Let me share my story with you:

I was born in Eagan, MN, where I was raised by Tom and Rosie Donnohue. I am an only child and enjoy filmmaking, writing, and playing trombone.

I am an alumni of Black Hawk Middle School and a graduate of Eastview High School in Apple Valley, Minnesota. I currently work as an Intern at Arcon Solutions, Inc. in Burnsville, MN.
