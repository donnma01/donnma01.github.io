Mason's Hobbies: Trombones Are Cool

There are many variants of trombones:

  • Piccolo Trombone
  • Soprano Trombone
  • Alto Trombone
  • Tenor Trombone
  • Bass Trombone*
  • Contrabass Trombone

*I play this instrument!

The Trombone is a brass instrument that was born in the 1400's. Since then, it has brought immense joy to musicians around the world.

Did you know? The first Trombone songs were written by Franz Joseph Haydn and Wolfgang Amadeus Mozart.

Some of the best trombone players known to man:

  1. Christian Lindberg
  2. Charlie Vernon
  3. Joseph Alessi

"No More Blues" - a Trombone Quartet piece I had the pleasure of playing once.
